Nutrition is the process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth. 
Every food contains macronutrients and micronutrients. Macronutrients include carbohydrates, proteins, and fats. 
Micronutrients include vitamins and minerals that support body functions.

Carbohydrates are the body's main energy source. They are found in grains, fruits, and vegetables. 
Complex carbohydrates such as oats and brown rice provide long-lasting energy and help in digestion.

Proteins help build and repair tissues. High-quality protein sources include eggs, fish, chicken, lentils, beans, and nuts. 
Fats are necessary for hormone production and energy storage. Healthy fats include olive oil, avocado, and nuts.

Vitamins and minerals play specific roles such as strengthening bones, improving immunity, and supporting metabolism. 
A balanced diet ensures that the body receives adequate nutrients for proper function.
